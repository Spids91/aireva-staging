Important Notice

Aireva Medic is a learning tool only.

This app is designed to support study, revision, and exam preparation for Irish paramedic students.

It is not a clinical reference tool and must not be used to guide patient care decisions.

Clinical content presented here has not been independently verified or endorsed by any regulatory body. Always refer to current official clinical practice guidelines and your service's approved protocols when treating patients.

The developer accepts no responsibility for clinical decisions made on the basis of information contained in this app.

Welcome to Aireva Medic
Your study companion for Irish paramedic students. Practise OSCE scenarios, test yourself with quizzes, and master the drugs, doses and scopes, all in one place.
🏥
Practise OSCE scenarios
Generate a fresh OSCE station any time: a realistic patient with a full clinical picture, different every run. Run it solo or as a group, then reveal the diagnosis and Paramedic-scope management for the debrief.
🧠
Built to help you retain
Test yourself in Quiz mode, and the app makes every session smarter:
🔁 Spaced repetitionTap to learn more
Revisit drugs just before you forget them
Every question you answer is remembered. Drugs you already know will appear less often. Drugs you struggle with come back sooner. Over time the app focuses your study where it's needed most.
📈 Adaptive difficultyTap to learn more
Gets harder as your knowledge grows
Early questions focus on indications and dosages. As your mastery grows the questions shift to mechanisms, contraindications and side effects.
📅 Daily ChallengeTap to learn more
Five questions every day to stay sharp
A short daily quiz that earns you bonus XP. Everyone gets the same questions each day. Complete it to build your streak, miss a day and the streak resets.
📈
Track your mastery
Earn XP, build streaks and watch every drug progress from Novice to Mastered. Over 700 questions across drugs and medical terms, all in one place.
